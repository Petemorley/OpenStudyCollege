Why Choose Open Study College?

As one of the UK's leading distance learning colleges, over 73,000 people have studied a home learning course with us and improved their career prospects.

Flexible & Convenient

We put you in control of your learning. You decide when, where and how you study. Unlike other colleges, there are no timetables or deadlines, so you can go at your own pace. Our flexible courses are designed to easily fit around your lifestyle, allowing you to finish your qualification in your own time.

Affordable Study

Our affordable and competitive prices allow you to invest in your future without worry. Our flexible monthly payment plans enable you to spread the cost of learning into stress-free, easy payments.

Unlimited Tutor Support

You are not alone when you choose to study with Open Study College. Our qualified personal tutors will provide the guidance and support you need throughout your studies to help you achieve your qualification.

STUDENT PERKS

Once you have enrolled with us, you will be entitled to a free NUS Extra card, allowing you to enjoy a variety of exclusive discounts. And that’s not all, upon completion of your course, our recruitment experts can help you to re-write your CV to ensure that your new skills help you to stand out to a potential employer. We want your studies to help you progress, so this is a complimentary service we provide.

A-LEVELS

We offer a broad range of A Levels that provide the necessary UCAS points needed for university, and act as a foundation to pursue the next stage of your career. We have a selection of 18 A Level subjects for you to choose from, including core subjects such as Maths, Biology, English Language and Literature and Chemistry, as well as Psychology, Business Studies, History and Sociology to name a few.

Cache

Our range of qualifications from CACHE, the UK’s leading awarding organisation for the care and education industries, cover areas such as teaching support, childcare, nutrition, and health and social care. These highly accredited qualifications will give you the skills you need to further your career.

Aat

The Association of Accounting Technicians is one of the leading organisations for accounting professionals. Our AAT qualifications range from beginner to advanced levels in both bookkeeping and accounting. Their accounting qualifications provide you with valuable knowledge and practical skills that employers will be looking for.

Active iq

Our courses from Active IQ - the UK’s leading organisation for vocational and active learning - provide professionally designed qualifications that promote careers in the active leisure and wellbeing sectors. Our course range includes fitness instruction, personal training and designing exercise programmes.
